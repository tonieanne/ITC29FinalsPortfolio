Removing excuses







“If it’s important to you, you’ll find a way.
If not, you’ll find an excuse.”


– Unknown





“The real man is one who always finds excuses for others,
but never excuses himself. ”


– Henry Ward Beecher





“Ninety-nine percent of the failures come from people
who have the habit of making excuses.”


– George Washington Carver





“It’s not the time to look for excuses.”


– Rafael Nadal





“The day you take complete responsibility for yourself,
the day you stop making any excuses,
that’s the day you start to the top.”


– O.J. Simpson
